Living at 425 Mass

At 425 Mass Apartments, you'll find amazing amenities, first-class services and an unrivaled residential luxury living experience. Located in the Mt. Vernon Triangle, 425 Mass is downtown Washington DC's first Smoke Free apartment community. Originally constructed as condominiums, the community's finishes are elegant and the floorplans are astonishingly spacious. Clear views of the skyline and the Washington Monument further augment the incredible experience. In addition to all of the neighborhood's shopping and dining destinations, residents also enjoy access to the rooftop swimming pools, a 24 Hour concierge service and lush garden retreats. There's something special for everyone at 425 Mass Apartments--the pinnacle of luxury living.

Mt. Vernon Triangle

425 Mass is located in the vibrant Mount Vernon Triangle neighborhood, a growing area that's accessible to public transit and nearby highways. Our neighborhood has a unique look to it, too, mixing modern and historic architectures and landmarks, and we're within walking distance to some of D.C.'s most diverse businesses and restaurants. If you want to live within walking distance to Chinatown or a short metro ride from Georgetown, Downtown D.C. and Dupont Circle and experience all that these areas have to offer, then Mount Vernon Triangle is the perfect area for you to call home. Visit our dynamic neighborhood today and experience the best that D.C. has to offer!
